Me.J uses your webcam to see and interpret your hand gestures. Please grant camera access when prompted by your browser to begin. Use your device webcam or an external camera for better accuracy. Make sure the camera is steady and positioned well for gesture detection.
Import your own tracks or explore Me.J’s online library. Whether it’s your personal beats or sample sounds, get your music ready for live mixing.
Position yourself so your hands are clearly visible within the webcam feed shown on screen. Good lighting and a relatively plain background will help the tracking work best. Make sure your movements stay within the camera's view.
